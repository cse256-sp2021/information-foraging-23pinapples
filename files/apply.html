Application Procedure

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

To apply for admission at WUNS, follow the following steps.

  1. Fill out an Application for Admissions Form either online or on PDF along with an application fee of $40.
  2. After your application is submitted, your child will be placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date.
  3. To accept your acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one month's tuition is due. This will be applied to tuition for the following May. You also need to fill out Developmental Form.
  4. To learn more about what forms are required for new students and current students, visit forms.
